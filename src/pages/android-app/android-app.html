then click Settings—the 4th

icon from the right

4. Tap Profiles

5. Tap Test portal to modify the existing

profile or Add profile to create a new one

6. Tap STB Model.

7. Tap MAG 250, or whichever STB

you wish to emulate

8. Tap Portal settings

9. Tap Portal URL

10. Enter your Portal for MAG and

STB: http://premium-iptv.link:6969/c

you need to buy or ask iptv

trial first and press OK

11. Now tap Screen Resolution

12. We recommend you choose

1280×720. You are welcome

to choose higher, but it may be laggy.

13. MOST IMPORTANT STEP: Scroll down

and find the MAC address.

This is an important number because

it is how your IPTV service will

identify your set-top box.

14. You must tell us your MAC address.

Done IPTV for Android IPTV STB

Emulator. Once you get an email

saying your order’s been completed
